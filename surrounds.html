Spa Surrounds

Cal Metro™ Surrounds includes convenient independent, movable components, which can be used to create hundreds of different hot tub décor options for your patio or backyard. Bars, barstools and privacy screens will transform your Cal Spas hot tub into the ultimate entertainment destination for family and friends. Create the backyard spa retreat of your dreams with the Cal Metro™ Surrounds.

Each portable Cal Metro™ piece can be used as an extension to other Cal Metro™ parts to expand the possibilities of outdoor furniture pieces. Don’t be limited to your hot tub area, place two bars side-by-side to create an outdoor picnic table anywhere in your yard, or place benches near one another to create a convivial space for family and friends to gather. With Cal Metro™ the possibilities are endless!
